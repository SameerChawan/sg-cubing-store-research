Sole Proprietorship Registration

ACRA (BizFile+) · $100 SGD · 1 day

The simplest business structure in Singapore. Your wife (DP holder) will be the registered owner. This is the first step before anything else.

Prerequisites

  • → Valid Dependent Pass (DP)
  • → SingPass account (DP holders can register for SingPass)
  • → Local residential address

Step-by-Step Procedure

Step 1: Log in to BizFile+ → Click "Log in" → Select "SingPass" → Enter credentials and complete 2FA

Step 2: From dashboard, click "Start a New Business" → Select "Sole Proprietorship"

Step 3: Fill in Business Details:

• Business Name: e.g., "SG Cubing Co." (check availability first)

• Principal Activity: "Retail sale of games and toys" (SSIC: 47620)

• Secondary Activity: "Retail sale via internet" (SSIC: 47910)

• Business Address: Home address (HDB flat)

• Start Date: Date you want business to start

Step 4: Fill in Owner Details: Full name (as per DP), NRIC/FIN, DOB, nationality, address

Step 5: Declare No Disqualifications: Confirm not undischarged bankrupt, not disqualified

Step 6: Review all information → Tick declaration → Click "Submit"

Step 7: Make Payment: $100 SGD (1-year registration) via Credit/Debit card or PayNow

Step 8: Download Certificate of Registration and Business Profile (free for 30 days) — save as PDF

Important: Registration is valid for 1 year — must renew annually ($30 SGD). Must update ACRA within 14 days if any details change. Business name reservation is optional ($15 SGD) but recommended.

Documents to Keep

✓ Certificate of Registration (PDF) ✓ Business Profile (PDF) ✓ Payment receipt
2

Letter of Consent (LOC)

MOM (EP Online) · Free · 3-5 weeks

A Letter of Consent from MOM allows a Dependent Pass holder to start and operate a business in Singapore. Without LOC, operating a business on DP is illegal and may result in pass cancellation.

Prerequisites

  • → Valid Dependent Pass (DP)
  • → Registered Sole Proprietorship (from Step 1)
  • → Employer sponsor (the business entity itself)

Step-by-Step Procedure

Step 1: Log in to EP Online → Click "Log in" → Select "SingPass"

Step 2: From dashboard, select "Apply for LOC" → Select "For Dependent Pass holder starting own business"

Step 3: Fill in Application Details:

• Applicant Details: Auto-filled from SingPass

• Business Name: "SG Cubing Co." (as registered with ACRA)

• UEN: Will be auto-assigned (see Step 4)

• Job Title: "Owner / Sole Proprietor"

• Monthly Salary: $0 (owner, not employee)

• Job Description: "Managing online retail business for speedcubes and accessories"

Step 4: Upload Supporting Documents:

• Copy of Dependent Pass (front & back)

• Certificate of Registration from ACRA

• Business Profile from ACRA

• Passport biodata page

• Educational certificates (highest qualification)

Step 5: Review all details → Tick declaration boxes → Click "Submit"

Step 6: Wait for Processing: 3-5 weeks — MOM may request additional documents — check status regularly

Step 7: If approved: Download LOC letter (PDF). If rejected: MOM will state reasons; can appeal within 30 days

Step 8: Download & print LOC letter — keep both digital and physical copies

Critical: LOC is tied to the DP — if DP expires/renews, LOC must be re-applied. Processing may take longer during peak periods.

Documents to Keep

✓ Letter of Consent (PDF + printed) ✓ Application confirmation receipt ✓ Any MOM correspondence

CorpPass + UEN Setup

ACRA · Free · Auto-assigned / 1 day

UEN (Unique Entity Number) is your business identification number (like a business NRIC). CorpPass is your corporate digital identity for government transactions.

Step-by-Step Procedure

Step 1: UEN is automatically assigned when you register with ACRA — found on your Certificate of Registration (format: e.g., "53123456A")

Step 2: Go to CorpPass → Click "Register as CorpPass Admin" → Log in with SingPass

Step 3: Enter UEN → Verify business details (auto-populated from ACRA) → Confirm admin details

Step 4: Set up Admin Account (yourself as DP holder) and optionally User Account (if you have helpers)

Step 5: Link Government Services:

• Link to IRAS (for tax filing)

• Link to Customs (for import permits)

• Link to ACRA (for annual filings)

Step 6: Download CorpPass mobile app (optional) → Save login credentials securely

Important: UEN never changes — use this number for all government interactions. Keep CorpPass credentials secure — it's your digital business identity.

GST Registration (IRAS)

IRAS myTax Portal · Free · 10 days

GST registration is mandatory only when annual turnover exceeds $1 million SGD. For SG Cubing Co., this is not needed initially (Year 1 projection: ~$21,000). Monitor quarterly.

When to Register

  • → Mandatory: When taxable turnover exceeds $1M in past 12 months
  • → Voluntary: Can register early if beneficial (usually not for small businesses)
  • → Recommendation: Register when approaching $800K threshold (to allow buffer)

Step-by-Step Procedure (When Needed)

Step 1: Log in to myTax Portal → Log in with CorpPass

Step 2: Navigate to "Register for GST" → Select "Compulsory GST Registration"

Step 3: Provide: UEN, business name, date of liability, bank account details

Step 4: Submit: Financial statements, sales records showing turnover exceeds $1M, Business Profile

Step 5: Await approval (~10 working days) — IRAS may request additional info

Step 6: Once registered: Must charge 9% GST on all sales, must file GST returns quarterly

Penalty: Late registration: 10% of GST due + $200 fine. Once registered, you must charge 9% GST on all taxable supplies.

PDPA Compliance

PDPC · Free · Before launch

The Personal Data Protection Act (PDPA) requires businesses to protect customer personal data. Non-compliance penalties: up to $1 million SGD or 10% of annual turnover.

What You Must Do

✓ Must Have

• Privacy Policy on website

• Consent checkbox at checkout

• Data access/correction process

• Security measures (HTTPS, passwords)

✗ Must Not

• Collect data without consent

• Share data without permission

• Retain data longer than necessary

• Ignore data access requests

Step-by-Step Procedure

Step 1: Create Privacy Policy — must include: what data you collect, why, how you use it, who you share with, how to access/correct data

Step 2: Add Privacy Policy page on Shopify → Link in footer → Link during checkout

Step 3: Add consent checkboxes: "I agree to Privacy Policy" + "Subscribe to newsletter" (separate)

Step 4: Create email for data requests: privacy@sgcubing.com → Process requests within 30 days

Step 5: Implement security: HTTPS (Shopify provides), strong passwords, limit access, regular updates

Sample Privacy Policy: "We collect your name, email, address, and phone for order fulfillment. We share with logistics partners (Ninja Van) and payment processors. Contact privacy@sgcubing.com for data requests."

Import Permit (Singapore Customs)

TradeNet · $2-8 SGD · Same day

Required for importing goods when shipment value exceeds $400 SGD. Key advantage: 0% import duty on toys — you only pay GST on imports (9% of CIF value, claimable if GST-registered).

HS Code for Speedcubes

9503.00 — Toys, games, and sports requisites → 0% import duty

Step-by-Step Procedure (DIY)

Step 1: Register as importer on TradeNet → Log in with CorpPass

Step 2: Apply for Customs Account → Provide UEN and business details → Processing: 1-2 days

Step 3: Prepare Documents: Commercial Invoice, Packing List, Bill of Lading/Airway Bill from supplier

Step 4: Submit Import Declaration on TradeNet → Enter HS Code: 9503.00 → Enter shipment value

Step 5: Make Payment: No duty (0%) + GST on import (9% of CIF) + Declaration fee (~$2-8)

Step 6: Approval usually within minutes → Download permit → Forward to freight forwarder

Recommended: Use a Freight Forwarder

For simplicity, use a freight forwarder who handles customs clearance:

• They submit the import declaration on your behalf

• They handle all documentation

• They deliver goods to your door

• Additional cost: ~$20-50 SGD per shipment

Recommended: Janio, DHL eCommerce, Ninja Direct

For shipments under $400: No import permit needed. Keep records for 5 years (IRAS requirement).

HDB · Free · No application needed

Perfect for lean operations: No license required. No fee. No approval wait time. Just comply with conditions and start selling.
