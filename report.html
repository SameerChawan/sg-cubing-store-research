5. Regulatory & Legal Framework

5.1 The Constraint

Under Singapore's Employment of Foreign Manpower Act (EFMA), EP and S Pass holders are prohibited from starting or operating a business. Violation may result in pass cancellation.

5.4 HDB Home-Based Business

Singapore allows small-scale home-based businesses in HDB flats without additional license, provided: no external employees, no nuisance, no signage. Ideal for lean e-commerce.

  • TikTok/Reels content 3x/week (satisfying solves, unboxing ASMR)
  • WCA competition sponsorship ($200-500 per event)
  • Influencer seeding — free cubes to SG micro-influencers
  • SEO: "buy speedcube Singapore", "best 3x3 cube 2026"
  • Target: 50-80 orders/month

Phase 2: Paid Acquisition (Month 3-9)

  • Meta ads (IG/FB): ages 13-35, $300-500/mo
  • Google Shopping: high-intent keywords, $200-300/mo
  • Shopee ads + flash sales, $100-200/mo
  • TikTok ads + live selling
  • Target: 150 orders/month

Phase 3: Scale & Expand (Month 10-18)

  • Loyalty program (points, referrals)
  • Subscription box: monthly mystery cube ($25-35/month)
  • Regional shipping: MY → TH → ID → PH

10. Key Takeaways

  1. Market gap is real and verified. No credible SG/SEA cube retailer exists. Cubewerkz is the only competitor and is structurally disadvantaged on cost.
  2. Supplier is de-risked. XingleToy offers MOQ 1, 1,923 SKUs, 26 brands, and prices verified via authenticated API.
  3. Margins are compelling. 45-70% gross margin on cubes. 70-84% on lubricants. 40.8% net per order.
  4. Capital required is minimal. SGD $5K-15K to launch. Break-even at 24 orders/month.
  5. Regulatory path is clear. DP holder + LOC + HDB home-based. Setup: $100 SGD.
  6. Regional upside is massive. 680M+ in SEA with no dominant cube retailer.
  7. Content is the moat. Build community, not just a store.
