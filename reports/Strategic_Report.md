## 5. Regulatory & Legal Framework

### 5.1 The Constraint

Under Singapore's **Employment of Foreign Manpower Act (EFMA)**, Employment Pass (EP) and S Pass holders are **prohibited from starting or operating a business**. Violation may result in pass cancellation and deportation.

### 5.2 The Solution: Dependent Pass + Letter of Consent

| Step | Action | Authority | Cost | Timeline |
|------|--------|-----------|------|----------|
| 1 | Register Sole Proprietorship (DP holder) | ACRA (BizFile+) | $100 | 1 day |
| 2 | Apply for Letter of Consent (LOC) | MOM (EPOL) | Free | 3-5 weeks |
| 3 | Open business bank account | DBS/OCBC/UOB | $0-50 | 1-2 weeks |
| 4 | Set up CorpPass + UEN | ACRA | Free | Auto-assigned |
| 5 | Register for GST (if >$1M turnover) | IRAS | Free | N/A initially |
| 6 | PDPA compliance (privacy policy) | PDPC | $0 | Before launch |
| 7 | Import permit (if >$400/shipment) | SG Customs | ~$20-50 | Per shipment |

### 5.3 EP Holder's Permitted Role

| ✅ CAN | ❌ CANNOT |
|--------|----------|
| Business strategy & planning | Be registered business owner |
| Product selection & sourcing | Sign contracts as principal |
| Website & technical support | Be listed as Sole Proprietor |
| Marketing strategy | Draw salary from business |
| Shareholder in Pte Ltd (later) | Be director without LOC |

### 5.4 HDB Home-Based Business

Singapore allows small-scale home-based businesses in HDB flats **without additional license**, provided:
- No external employees hired
- No nuisance to neighbours
- No signage displayed
- No alteration to flat structure

This is **ideal for a lean e-commerce operation** fulfilling orders from home.
